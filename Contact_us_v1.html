Project Novo is a non-profit start-up company that codes and creates modern websites for other non-profit organizations. The group is managed and supported by hard-working Silicon Valley high-schoolers with talents in coding and communication, which are the two main branches of the company. Students who are part of the coding branch will spend time designing unique websites, while students who are part of the communication or "outreach" branch will seek out organizations interested in giving Project Novo a chance to give their websites a fresh, new look.
